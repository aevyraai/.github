# Aevyra

**Open source AI infrastructure — built across the full LLM stack.**

</div>

---

Aevyra is an umbrella for open-source projects targeting every layer of what makes large language models actually work — from low-level GPU kernel optimizations all the way up to next-generation personal intelligence systems.

The goal isn't to wrap existing models. It's to make them faster, smarter to use, and genuinely more capable — by working at the layers most people don't touch.

## What We're Building

**Low-level performance**
Custom GPU kernels written in Triton for LLM attention, matmul, and quantization — pushing inference speed at the hardware level.

**Agentic tooling**
Infrastructure for automating the hard parts of the LLM development lifecycle — including agentic fine-tuning pipelines that curate data, train, evaluate, and iterate with minimal human intervention.

**Personal intelligence**
A new kind of personal assistant — not a chatbot with a longer context window, but a persistent, context-aware system that genuinely knows your work and acts on your behalf.
